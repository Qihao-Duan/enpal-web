## Style System

- Selected style pack: `neo-tech`
- Rationale: The repo is an AI/product/energy-tech prototype, and the slide needs a credible tech/startup feel while preserving trust, auditability, and presentation readability.
- Composed style source: `references/style-packs/base-core/00-core-foundation.md` + `references/style-packs/neo-tech/*`
- Style constraints:
  - One 16:9 widescreen slide image, no separate layers.
  - High-contrast text areas, readable at presentation distance.
  - Deep blue-gray base with controlled cyan/teal/violet accents.
  - Glassmorphism cards with thin glowing borders and subtle network nodes.
  - No random text, watermark, logo, QR code, or brand imitation.
  - Avoid Octopus visual identity, copy, mascot, fonts, or palette.
