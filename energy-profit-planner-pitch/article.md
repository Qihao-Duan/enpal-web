# Source Pitch Text

## §1 Title

Two-Minute Hackathon Pitch Script

Energy Profit Planner

## §2 Factory Surplus Problem

Imagine a medium-sized factory with a large solar rooftop system.

At noon, the factory produces more solar energy than it can use immediately. Because there is no intelligent planning layer, much of that surplus energy is sold back to the grid at a low price. But later in the afternoon or evening, when production machines, electric forklifts, cooling systems, and batteries need power, the same factory may have to buy electricity back from the grid at a much higher price.

So the real problem is not simply whether the factory has enough solar energy. The real problem is: where should every kilowatt-hour go, and when should it be used, stored, or sold to create the highest value?

## §3 Product Data Inputs

Our project is an Energy Profit Planner.

It connects to the factory's energy profile, including solar panels, batteries, electric vehicles, production equipment, heating and cooling systems, and historical consumption patterns. It can also read contract information, dynamic electricity prices, weather forecasts, and device specifications. Based on this data, our system creates an optimized energy plan for the next 24 hours, broken down into short time intervals.

## §4 Optimization Example

For example, if the system predicts strong solar generation at noon and higher electricity prices in the afternoon, it can recommend charging batteries and electric forklifts during the solar peak, shifting flexible equipment usage into low-cost windows, and saving stored energy for expensive periods. If market prices are attractive, the system can also decide when selling surplus electricity is more profitable than storing or using it internally.

The user does not just see raw energy data. They see a clear comparison: what their normal energy cost would be, what the optimized cost would be, and how much money they can save or earn.

## §5 Service Levels

We offer two service levels.

For regular users, the basic plan focuses on smart charging and appliance scheduling. The goal is to reduce electricity costs without hurting comfort or daily routines.

For premium users, such as factories or solar-powered households with batteries, we provide advanced solar trading and storage optimization. The goal is not only to save money, but to turn redundant solar energy into a predictable source of profit.

## §6 Closing

Our key idea is simple: we do not just monitor energy consumption. We actively plan energy usage and energy sales.

We help every kilowatt-hour move to the place where it is needed most, at the time when it creates the most economic value.
